Detailed Description

D0-D0bar correlation pair builder - for MC gen-level analysis, like sign particles NOTE: At the moment, both dPhi-symmetrical correlation pairs (part1-part2 and part2-part1) are filled, since we bin in pT and selecting as trigger the largest pT particle would bias the distributions w.r.t. the ULS case.
